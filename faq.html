Frequently Asked Questions

How to reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page and follow the instructions.

How to register for online banking?

Click on “New User Registration” and follow the steps shown on the screen.

What to do if ATM card is lost?

Immediately use the “Block ATM Card” link or call customer care.

Can I access SBI services on mobile?

Yes, through the YONO SBI app available on Android and iOS platforms.
